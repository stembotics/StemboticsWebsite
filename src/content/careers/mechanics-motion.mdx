### Course Overview
This course provides a comprehensive introduction to classical mechanics. Students will learn about kinematics, dynamics, and energy conservation, with a focus on understanding how objects move and interact under the influence of forces.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** Kinematics, Displacement, Velocity, Acceleration, Motion Graphs.
* **Week 2:** Newton's Laws of Motion, Force, Mass, and Acceleration, Free Body Diagrams.
* **Week 3:** Work and Energy, Work-Energy Theorem, Kinetic Energy, Potential Energy.
* **Week 4:** Conservation of Energy, Conservative and Non-conservative Forces, Power.
* **Week 5:** Momentum, Impulse, Conservation of Momentum, Collisions.
* **Week 6:** Rotational Motion, Torque, Angular Momentum, Moment of Inertia.
