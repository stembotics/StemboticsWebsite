### Course Overview

This introductory course covers the basic principles of rocketry, from Newton's laws of motion to the different types of rocket engines. Students will learn about the history of rocketry, the challenges of space travel, and the future of space exploration.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** History of Rocketry,  Newton's Laws of Motion, Principles of Rocket Propulsion.
* **Week 2:** Rocket Engines, Solid Rocket Motors, Liquid Rocket Engines.
* **Week 3:** Rocket Design and Construction,  Aerodynamics, Stability and Control.
* **Week 4:** Orbital Mechanics,  Getting to Space, Orbits and Trajectories.
* **Week 5:** Space Missions,  Past and Present Space Missions, Future of Space Exploration.
* **Week 6:** Building and Launching Model Rockets (Optional hands-on activity).
