### Course Overview

This course explores practical physics applications in technology and everyday life. We'll cover key concepts like motion, energy, forces, and waves, illustrating how these principles underpin various innovations. From smartphones to renewable energy, understand the physics behind the technology that shapes our world.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** Introduction to Physics and Measurement, Units and Dimensions, Motion in One Dimension.
* **Week 2:** Motion in Two Dimensions, Vectors, Projectile Motion, Circular Motion.
* **Week 3:** Newton's Laws of Motion, Force and Motion, Work and Energy.
* **Week 4:** Energy Conservation, Potential Energy, Kinetic Energy, Power.
* **Week 5:** Waves and Sound, Properties of Waves, Sound Waves, Doppler Effect.
* **Week 6:** Light and Optics, Reflection, Refraction, Lenses, Optical Instruments.
