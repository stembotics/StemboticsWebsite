### Course Overview

This course focuses on the design and implementation of smart home systems, covering various technologies and platforms used for home automation. Students will learn about smart lighting, security systems, and other connected devices for the home.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** Introduction to Smart Homes,  Smart Home Ecosystems.
* **Week 2:** Smart Lighting,  Controlling Lights with Your Smartphone,  Automated Lighting Schedules.
* **Week 3:** Smart Security Systems,  Connected Cameras,  Motion Sensors.
* **Week 4:**  Smart Appliances,  Controlling Appliances Remotely,  Energy Management.
* **Week 5:**  Voice Control and Assistants,  Integrating with Alexa or Google Assistant.
* **Week 6:**  Building a Smart Home Project (Optional hands-on activity).
