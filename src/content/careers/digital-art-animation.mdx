### Course Overview

This course introduces students to the exciting world of digital art and animation. We'll explore different software and techniques to create drawings, illustrations, and simple animations. Age-appropriate software and projects will be selected based on the students' skill levels.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** Introduction to Digital Art: Exploring different digital art tools and software. Basic drawing exercises.
* **Week 2:**  Drawing and Painting with Digital Tools: Learning to use brushes, colors, and layers in digital art software.
* **Week 3:**  Creating Digital Illustrations:  Developing a simple illustration project.
* **Week 4:**  Introduction to Animation:  Exploring the principles of animation through simple exercises.
* **Week 5:**  Creating Simple Animations:  Using animation software to create a short animated sequence.
* **Week 6:**  Sharing Your Art:  Showcasing student artwork and animations.
