### Course Overview
This course explores the foundations of genetics and evolutionary biology. Students will learn how genes are passed down through generations and how evolution shapes the natural world over time.

### Expected Curriculum
**Week 1:** Introduction to DNA and genetics: Structure and function of genes.  
**Week 2:** Heredity: How traits are passed from parents to offspring.  
**Week 3:** Mutations and genetic disorders.  
**Week 4:** Principles of natural selection and adaptation.  
**Week 5:** The fossil record and evolutionary history.  
**Week 6:** Final Project: Research paper or presentation on a genetic or evolutionary topic.

### What You Get

- 1 hour session, every week, for 6 weeks.
- Access to digital genetics tools and resources.
- Research guidance and feedback.
