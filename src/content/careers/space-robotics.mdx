### Course Overview

This course explores the fascinating field of space robotics, covering the design, construction, and operation of robots for various space missions. Students will learn about the challenges of operating robots in the harsh environment of space and the future of robotic space exploration.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** Introduction to Space Robotics, Types of Space Robots, Applications of Space Robots.
* **Week 2:** Designing Robots for Space, Materials and Electronics,  Power Systems.
* **Week 3:**  Robot Mobility and Manipulation,  Locomotion Systems, Robotic Arms and Grippers.
* **Week 4:**  Sensors and Perception,  Computer Vision, Navigation and Control.
* **Week 5:**  Communication and Control, Teleoperation, Autonomous Navigation.
* **Week 6:**  Case Studies of Space Robots,  Current and Future Missions.
