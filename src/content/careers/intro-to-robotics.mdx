### Course Overview
In this introductory course, students will learn the basics of robotics, including robot design, building, and programming. The course is designed for beginners with no prior experience required. By the end of the course, students will have built their own simple robot and programmed it to perform basic functions.

### Expected Curriculum

- **Week 1**: Introduction to Robotics and Basic Components
- **Week 2**: Robot Design Principles and Tools
- **Week 3**: Building Your First Robot
- **Week 4**: Introduction to Robot Programming (Using Arduino or Scratch)
- **Week 5**: Debugging and Improving the Robot's Functionality
- **Week 6**: Final Project: Build and Program a Simple Robot

### What You Get

- 1 hour session, every week, for 6 weeks
- Access to robot building kits for duration of the course
- Hands-on exercises and project guidance
