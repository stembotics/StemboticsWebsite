### Package Overview

The VEX Competition Package is an add-on to the VEX Learning Plan. It allows students to participate in the actual VEX IQ competitions, providing a platform to showcase their robotics skills on a larger stage.

### Requirements

- Must be enrolled in the VEX Learning Plan
- Need a team of 4-5 people

### What You Get

- Entry to VEX IQ competitions
- Additional preparation and support for competitions

### Pricing

- $150 per Season (year)
