### Course Overview
This course delves into the use of robotics and automation in manufacturing and industry. You'll explore how automation systems work, learn about industrial robots, and examine how automation improves efficiency in production processes.

### Expected Curriculum
**Week 1:** Introduction to industrial automation: History and trends.

**Week 2:** Understanding automation systems and controllers.

**Week 3:** Robotics in manufacturing: Exploring robotic arms and assembly lines.

**Week 4:** Programming industrial robots: Basics of automation software.

**Week 5:** Optimizing production through automation: Case studies.

**Week 6:** Final Project: Designing an automated solution for a given industry challenge.

### What You Get

- 1 hour session, every week, for 6 weeks.
- Access to automation and robotics software.
- Case studies and industry-based projects.
