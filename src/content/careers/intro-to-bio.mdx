### Course Overview
This introductory biology course covers fundamental topics like cell structure, genetics, and ecosystems. It's perfect for students who want a broad understanding of how life works at different scales, from the microscopic to the global.

### Expected Curriculum
**Week 1:** Introduction to cell biology: Understanding cells and organelles.  
**Week 2:** DNA and genetics: How traits are inherited.  
**Week 3:** Microorganisms and their role in the ecosystem.  
**Week 4:** Ecosystem dynamics and energy flow.  
**Week 5:** Genetics in action: Mutations and evolutionary processes.  
**Week 6:** Final Project: Research presentation on an ecological or genetic topic.

### What You Get

- 1 hour session, every week, for 6 weeks.
- Hands-on experiments and access to digital biology resources.
- Guided research and project assistance.
