### Course Overview

This course examines the use of IoT in industrial settings, covering applications in manufacturing, agriculture, healthcare, and transportation. Students will learn about industrial IoT sensors, data analytics, and the impact of IoT on different sectors.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:**  Industrial IoT (IIoT),  Applications of IIoT,  Benefits and Challenges.
* **Week 2:**  IoT in Manufacturing,  Predictive Maintenance,  Process Optimization.
* **Week 3:**  IoT in Agriculture,  Precision Agriculture,  Smart Farming.
* **Week 4:**  IoT in Healthcare,  Remote Patient Monitoring,  Wearable Medical Devices.
* **Week 5:**  IoT in Transportation,  Smart Cities,  Autonomous Vehicles.
* **Week 6:**  Future Trends in IIoT,  Edge Computing,  AI and Machine Learning in IoT.
