### Course Overview
This course explores the practical applications of mathematics in different technological domains. Students will learn how mathematical concepts are used to solve real-world problems in areas like computer graphics, cryptography, and machine learning.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** Introduction to Applied Mathematics, Number Systems, Algebra.
* **Week 2:** Calculus for Engineers, Derivatives, Integrals.
* **Week 3:** Linear Algebra, Vectors, Matrices.
* **Week 4:**  Discrete Mathematics, Logic, Set Theory.
* **Week 5:**  Probability and Statistics.
* **Week 6:**  Mathematical Modeling, Applications in Technology.
