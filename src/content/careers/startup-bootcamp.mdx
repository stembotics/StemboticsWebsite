### Course Overview
This intensive bootcamp provides a practical guide to starting a business. Students will learn about idea validation, market research, business planning, and fundraising.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** Idea Generation and Validation, Market Research, Identifying Your Target Audience.
* **Week 2:**  Business Planning,  Developing a Business Model Canvas,  Financial Projections.
* **Week 3:**  Marketing and Sales,  Building a Brand,  Customer Acquisition Strategies.
* **Week 4:**  Legal and Regulatory Considerations,  Choosing a Business Structure,  Intellectual Property.
* **Week 5:**  Fundraising,  Pitching to Investors,  Seed Funding and Venture Capital.
* **Week 6:**  Building a Team,  Leadership and Management,  Scaling Your Startup.
