### Course Overview

This course introduces the exciting world of space mission design. Students will learn about the different stages of planning a space mission, from defining objectives to designing spacecraft and planning trajectories. We'll use hands-on activities, simulations, and creative problem-solving to bring the wonders of space exploration to life.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** What is a Space Mission? Exploring different types of missions (e.g., orbiters, landers, flybys).
* **Week 2:** Destination: [Planet/Moon]! Choosing a target and researching its characteristics.
* **Week 3:** Designing Your Spacecraft: Building model spacecraft with specific functions in mind.
* **Week 4:** Planning the Trajectory:  Simple demonstrations of orbital mechanics and plotting a course.
* **Week 5:** Mission Challenges:  Problem-solving scenarios related to space travel (e.g., communication delays, resource management).
* **Week 6:**  Mission Presentation: Students present their mission plans.
