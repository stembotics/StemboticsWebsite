### Course Overview

This course provides a fun, engaging introduction to biochemistry for young learners. We'll explore the building blocks of life, like proteins, carbohydrates, and DNA, using hands-on activities, models, and simple experiments.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:**  The Chemistry of Life: What are living things made of?
* **Week 2:**  Proteins: Building blocks of our bodies. Building models of amino acids (simplified).
* **Week 3:**  Carbohydrates: The energy of life. Experiments with sugars and starches (e.g., testing foods for sugar).
* **Week 4:**  Fats and Lipids: Why are they important?  Simple demonstrations of the properties of fats and oils. 
* **Week 5:**  DNA: The secret code of life. Building a DNA model (simplified).
* **Week 6:**  The Chemistry of Food and Digestion:  Exploring how our bodies break down food into nutrients.
