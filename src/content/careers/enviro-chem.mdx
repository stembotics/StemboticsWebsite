### Course Overview
This course introduces the fundamental chemical processes occurring in the environment. We'll cover topics like water and air pollution, the chemistry of the atmosphere, and the impact of human activities on the environment. Students will gain an understanding of chemical analysis techniques used to monitor environmental quality and explore solutions to pressing environmental challenges.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** Introduction to Environmental Chemistry, Basic Concepts and Terminology, Chemical Analysis Techniques.
* **Week 2:** The Chemistry of the Atmosphere, Air Pollution, Ozone Depletion, and Acid Rain.
* **Week 3:** Water Chemistry, Water Pollution, Water Treatment and Purification Methods.
* **Week 4:** Soil Chemistry, Contamination and Remediation, Waste Management and Disposal.
* **Week 5:**  Environmental Toxicology, Effects of Pollutants on Living Organisms, Risk Assessment.
* **Week 6:** Climate Change, Greenhouse Gases,  Sustainable Practices, and Future of Environmental Chemistry.
