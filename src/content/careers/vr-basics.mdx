### Course Overview
This introductory course provides a foundational understanding of virtual reality (VR), covering the hardware, software, and techniques used to create immersive VR experiences. Students will explore different VR platforms and development tools.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** Introduction to Virtual Reality, Types of VR Systems, VR Hardware.
* **Week 2:** VR Software and Development Tools,  Setting up a VR Development Environment.
* **Week 3:** Creating 3D Environments for VR,  3D Modeling Basics.
* **Week 4:** Interaction in VR,  User Interface Design,  Motion Tracking.
* **Week 5:**  VR Application, Gaming, etc
