### Course Overview
This course introduces the core principles of mechanical engineering, covering topics such as statics, dynamics, and material science. Participants will gain hands-on experience designing and prototyping mechanical systems.

### Expected Curriculum
**Week 1:** Introduction to mechanical engineering and design principles.  
**Week 2:** Statics and forces: Understanding the basics of mechanical forces.  
**Week 3:** Materials and their properties: Selecting the right materials for your design.  
**Week 4:** Prototyping basics: Building your first mechanical system.  
**Week 5:** Testing and refining your design: Ensuring stability and functionality.  
**Week 6:** Final Project: Build and test a working mechanical prototype.

### What You Get

- 1 hour session, every week, for 6 weeks.
- Access to prototyping materials and tools.
- Personalized guidance on your mechanical design project.
