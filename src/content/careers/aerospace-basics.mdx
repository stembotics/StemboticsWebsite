### Course Overview

This course provides a fun and engaging introduction to aerospace engineering. Students will explore the principles of flight, design and build simple flying machines, and learn about the forces that act on aircraft and spacecraft.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** The Magic of Flight: Exploring how things fly through demonstrations and simple experiments.
* **Week 2:**  Forces of Flight: Introduction to lift, drag, thrust, and weight. Building paper airplanes and testing different designs.
* **Week 3:**  Building and Launching Rockets: Simple rocketry activities (with appropriate safety measures).
* **Week 4:**  Aerodynamics:  Experimenting with different shapes and their effect on air resistance.
* **Week 5:**  Spacecraft Design:  Designing and building model spacecraft for different missions.
* **Week 6:**  Flight Simulators and Virtual Tours:  Experiencing flight through simulations and virtual reality (if available).
