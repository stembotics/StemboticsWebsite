### Course Overview

This course provides a comprehensive overview of the musculoskeletal system, including the structure and function of bones, muscles, and joints. Students will learn about different types of movement, muscle contractions, and the biomechanics of human motion.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** Introduction to the Musculoskeletal System, Bone Structure and Function.
* **Week 2:**  Types of Bones, Bone Growth and Development.
* **Week 3:** Muscle Tissue, Types of Muscles, Muscle Contraction.
* **Week 4:** Joints, Types of Joints, Joint Movement and Stability.
* **Week 5:** Biomechanics of Movement, Levers, Forces, and Motion.
* **Week 6:** Common Musculoskeletal Injuries and Disorders.
