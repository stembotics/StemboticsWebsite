### Course Overview

This course provides a fun, age-appropriate introduction to chemical engineering. Students will learn how chemical engineers design processes to create everything from candy to clean energy through hands-on experiments and design challenges.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** What is Chemical Engineering?  Introduction to the concept of transforming materials.
* **Week 2:**  Separating Mixtures:  Simple experiments like filtering, evaporation, and chromatography.
* **Week 3:**  Chemical Reactions:  Safe and engaging experiments demonstrating chemical changes (e.g., baking soda and vinegar).
* **Week 4:**  Designing a Process:  A simple design challenge related to a real-world problem (e.g., purifying water).
* **Week 5:**  Chemical Engineering in Everyday Life:  Exploring examples of chemical engineering in different industries.
* **Week 6:**  Building a Mini Chemical Plant (a simplified, hands-on activity).
