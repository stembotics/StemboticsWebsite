### Plan Overview

The FLL Learning Plan at Stembotics provides a hands-on robotics learning experience using the LEGO Spike Prime kit. Students will attend weekly sessions to build and program their robots.

### Competitions

Participants will have the chance to compete in organization-wide competitions every 2 months, enabling them to test their skills and collaborate with other teams.

### What You Get

- Weekly robotics learning sessions with LEGO Spike Prime kit
- Hands-on experience with building and programming
- Bi-monthly competitions
