### Course Overview

This course introduces the concept of mathematical modeling using age-appropriate examples and activities. Students will learn how to translate real-world problems into mathematical equations and use math to find solutions.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** What is a Model? Building Simple Models with Objects.
* **Week 2:**  Using Drawings and Diagrams to Represent Problems.
* **Week 3:**  Introduction to Variables and Equations (simple examples).
* **Week 4:**  Using Basic Arithmetic to Solve Problems.
* **Week 5:**  Building Models with Shapes and Patterns.
* **Week 6:**  Real-World Modeling Challenges and Games.
