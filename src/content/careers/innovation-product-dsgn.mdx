### Course Overview

This course explores the process of developing innovative products, from initial concept to final design. Students will learn about design thinking, user research, prototyping, and user testing.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** Design Thinking, The Design Process, Understanding User Needs.
* **Week 2:** Ideation and Brainstorming, Generating Creative Product Ideas.
* **Week 3:**  Prototyping, Creating Low-Fidelity and High-Fidelity Prototypes.
* **Week 4:** User Testing, Gathering Feedback, Iterative Design.
* **Week 5:** Product Development, Manufacturing Processes, Bringing Your Product to Market.
* **Week 6:**  Case Studies in Product Design,  Successful Product Launches.
