### Course Overview

This introductory astrophysics course provides a foundational understanding of the universe, from the properties of stars and galaxies to the mysteries of dark matter and dark energy. Students will explore the life cycle of stars, the formation of galaxies, and the evolution of the cosmos.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** Introduction to Astrophysics, The Scale of the Universe,  Observational Astronomy.
* **Week 2:** Stars,  Stellar Evolution,  The Life Cycle of Stars.
* **Week 3:** Galaxies,  Types of Galaxies, The Milky Way Galaxy.
* **Week 4:** Cosmology, The Big Bang Theory, The Expanding Universe.
* **Week 5:**  Dark Matter and Dark Energy,  The Mysteries of the Universe.
* **Week 6:**  Exoplanets and the Search for Life,  The Future of Astrophysics.
