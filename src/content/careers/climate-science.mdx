### Course Overview

This course explores the science behind climate change, covering the Earth's climate system, greenhouse gases, and the impacts of human activities on the climate. Students will examine the evidence for climate change, its consequences, and potential solutions.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** The Earth's Climate System,  Climate vs. Weather,  Climate History.
* **Week 2:** Greenhouse Gases,  The Greenhouse Effect,  Human Impact on the Atmosphere.
* **Week 3:** Evidence for Climate Change,  Temperature Records,  Ice Core Data.
* **Week 4:** Consequences of Climate Change,  Sea Level Rise,  Extreme Weather Events.
* **Week 5:** Climate Models and Projections,  Future Climate Scenarios.
* **Week 6:**  Climate Change Solutions,  Renewable Energy,  Climate Policy.
