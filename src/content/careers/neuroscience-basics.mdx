### Course Overview

This introductory neuroscience course covers the basic principles of nervous system organization and function. Students will learn about neurons, synapses, neurotransmitters, and the different regions of the brain.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** Introduction to Neuroscience, Cells of the Nervous System, Neuron Structure and Function.
* **Week 2:** Synaptic Transmission, Neurotransmitters, Neural Communication.
* **Week 3:** The Central Nervous System, Brain Anatomy, Brain Regions and Functions.
* **Week 4:** The Peripheral Nervous System, Sensory and Motor Neurons, Reflexes.
* **Week 5:**  The Senses, Vision, Hearing, Taste, Smell, Touch.
* **Week 6:**  Brain Disorders and Diseases,  Current Research in Neuroscience.
