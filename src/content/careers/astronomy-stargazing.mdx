### Course Overview

This course introduces students to the fascinating world of astronomy. We'll explore the solar system, stars, constellations, and galaxies through interactive activities, presentations, and (weather permitting) stargazing sessions.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** Our Solar System: Learning about the planets and other objects in our solar system. Building a scale model of the solar system.
* **Week 2:** Stars and Constellations: Learning to identify major constellations and bright stars. Creating star charts.
* **Week 3:** Galaxies and the Universe:  Exploring the vastness of space and the different types of galaxies.
* **Week 4:**  Telescopes and Space Exploration:  Learning how astronomers use telescopes to study the universe.
* **Week 5:**  The Moon and its Phases: Observing the moon and understanding its cycles.
* **Week 6:**  Stargazing Night (weather permitting):  Putting our knowledge into practice and observing the night sky.
