### Course Overview

This course covers the fundamental principles of electricity, magnetism, and their interconnectedness. We'll explore electric charges, circuits, magnetic fields, and electromagnetic induction, laying the foundation for understanding various electrical and electronic devices.

### Expected Curriculum

* **Week 1:** Electric Charges and Fields, Coulomb's Law, Electric Field, Electric Potential.
* **Week 2:** Electric Circuits, Current, Voltage, Resistance, Ohm's Law.
* **Week 3:** Magnetism, Magnetic Fields, Magnetic Force, Magnetic Materials.
* **Week 4:** Electromagnetic Induction, Faraday's Law, Lenz's Law, Inductance.
* **Week 5:** AC Circuits, Alternating Current, Capacitors, Inductors, Resonance.
* **Week 6:** Electromagnetic Waves, Properties of Electromagnetic Waves, Electromagnetic Spectrum.
