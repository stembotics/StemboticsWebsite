### Course Overview
In this course, participants will explore the basics of electrical engineering, including how to design and build simple circuits, solder components, and program microcontrollers. No prior experience is required.

### Expected Curriculum
**Week 1:** Introduction to electricity and basic circuit components.  
**Week 2:** Designing simple circuits: Resistors, capacitors, and LEDs.  
**Week 3:** Soldering techniques: How to build lasting connections.  
**Week 4:** Introduction to microcontrollers: Arduino basics.  
**Week 5:** Programming microcontrollers: Building a simple project.  
**Week 6:** Final Project: Build and demonstrate an electrical system.

### What You Get

- 1 hour session, every week, for 6 weeks.
- Access to circuit components, microcontrollers, and soldering tools.
- Project-based mentorship.
