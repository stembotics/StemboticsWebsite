### Course Overview
This course explores the field of materials science, focusing on how the properties of materials influence their use in engineering, manufacturing, and everyday products. Participants will learn about metals, polymers, ceramics, and more.

### Expected Curriculum
**Week 1:** Introduction to materials science: Types of materials and their uses.  
**Week 2:** Properties of metals: Strength, ductility, and conductivity.  
**Week 3:** Polymers and plastics: Understanding synthetic materials.  
**Week 4:** Ceramics and composites: Special applications and properties.  
**Week 5:** Testing and analyzing materials: Hands-on experiments.  
**Week 6:** Final Project: Selecting and analyzing materials for a specific application.

### What You Get

- 1 hour session, every week, for 6 weeks.
- Access to materials testing equipment.
- Sample projects and experiments.
