Explore advanced robotics concepts such as AI integration, autonomous navigation, and more complex robotic systems. Participants will learn how to apply cutting-edge technologies in real-world scenarios, focusing on industrial and practical uses.

**Week 1:** Overview of advanced robotics: Understanding the key areas of development.
