### Course Overview
This course teaches the fundamentals of 3D modeling and printing, enabling participants to design and print their own objects. Students will explore different types of 3D printers, software, and materials used in additive manufacturing.

### Expected Curriculum
**Week 1:** Introduction to 3D printing technology and software.  
**Week 2:** Basics of 3D modeling: Creating simple designs in CAD software.  
**Week 3:** Refining your designs: Adding details and optimizing for printing.  
**Week 4:** Preparing files for 3D printing: Understanding slicing software.  
**Week 5:** Printing your design: Using a 3D printer to bring your model to life.  
**Week 6:** Final Project: Showcasing a 3D printed object of your own design.

### What You Get

- 1 hour session, every week, for 6 weeks.
- Access to 3D printers and CAD software.
- Printing materials for one project.
